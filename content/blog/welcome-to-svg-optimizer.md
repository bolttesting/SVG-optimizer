## Why SVG optimization matters

SVGs are perfect for icons and illustrations, but exported files often include **metadata**, **comments**, and **over-precise paths** that inflate file size.

Our tool runs **SVGO**-style optimization so you can:

- **Preview** before and after
- **Tune** precision and which plugins apply
- **Batch** multiple files and download a ZIP

## Privacy

Your files are processed on your device for the interactive tool; when you use server optimization, only that request hits our API—never shared with third parties for ads.
